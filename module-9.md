Inputs

- Cost data per process/product – Expenses from each step of production or for each product line.
- Supplier financial data – Prices, delivery costs, and comparative metrics collected from Module 4.
- Machine cost profiles – TCO and operational costs per machine from Module 6.
- Operational KPIs – Data from Digital Twin (Module 8), like downtime cost, efficiency, and production rates.

---

Core Calculations

- Aggregate costs per process/product – Total expenses grouped by production process or product family.
- Supplier comparisons – Ranking and scoring suppliers based on cost, delivery, and quality.
- Machine performance costs – Cost efficiency metrics per machine, including TCO breakdowns.
- Cost trends & summaries – Identify patterns, high-cost areas, and potential savings.

---

Outputs

- Exportable PDFs – Reports for management, investors, or stakeholders.
- Dashboards – Interactive visualizations of costs, supplier rankings, and machine efficiency.
- Summary tables – Concise cost breakdowns for quick decision-making.
- Charts & KPIs – Graphical insights for financial and operational performance.

---

Usage / Integration

- Supports strategic decisions – Helps management optimize suppliers, machines, and processes.
- Feeds business plan (Module 7) – Provides aggregated cost data for projections and ROI calculations.
- Integrates with Digital Twin (Module 8) – Combines real-time operational costs with historical data.
